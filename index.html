Brain to Genetics Mappings of Disorder

Neuro2Gene is a mapping between functional brain networks, as summarized in the neuroscience literature via the NeuroSynth database, and genes, selected with intelligent subset selection using data from the Allen Brain Atlas. We generate 525 gene sets that describe psychologically meaningful terms "brainterms," and use Gene Set Enrichment Analysis to find which of our gene sets are enriched in disorder phenotype. Enrichment means that we have found genes that might be involved with the manifestation of some behavior related to the functional brain maps, a unique mapping from brain, behavior, to gene that could discover new genetic targets for diagnosis and treatment.
